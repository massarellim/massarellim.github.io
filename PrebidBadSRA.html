Prebid SRA Bottleneck Demonstration

This page simulates a common anti-pattern where Single Request Architecture (SRA) is enabled, but one bidder is extremely slow. Because GPT waits for the Prebid auction to complete before sending the single request for all slots, the slow bidder holding up the auction blocks the delivery of all ads on the page, including those that had winning bids ready in hundreds of milliseconds.
